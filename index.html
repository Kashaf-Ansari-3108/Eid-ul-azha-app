EID-UL-ADHA MUBARAK

تَقَبَّلَ اللهُ مِنَّا وَمِنكُم

May the pious occasion of Eid-ud-Adha brings prosperity, joy and positivity in your life. Eid Mubarak to you and your family.

By' Kashaf Ansari

Qurbani (Sacrifice)

Qurbani is an obligation that we are commanded to perform by Allah (SWT)

The time for offering the sacrifice begins after the Special Eid-ul-Adha Prayer on 10th of Dhul Hijjah and ends on 12th of Dhul Hijjah
